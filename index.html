Welcome To CSI,
Hospital

In Bangalore, Church of South India Hospital is a recognized name in patient care. It was incepted in the year 1893. They are one of the well-known Private Hospitals in HKP Road. Backed with a vision to offer the best in patient care and equipped with technologically advanced healthcare facilities, they are one of the upcoming names in the healthcare industry. The hospital is under Karnataka central diocese and present building of 240 beds with all the modern amenities was inaugurated in 1987.
